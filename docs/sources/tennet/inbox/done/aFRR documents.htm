About TenneT

TenneT keeps the Netherlands switched on. We transport electricity 24/7 via the high-voltage grid, connect customers and facilitate the energy transition by expanding the grid and using it smartly. Together with our partners, we work towards a reliable, affordable and resilient energy system. By doing so, we connect the Netherlands to a sustainable future. Connecting everyone to a brighter energy future.
